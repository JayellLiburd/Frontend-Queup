FAQs

  • How do I download my Enrollment Certificate?

    Click on “Download Enrollment Certificate,” then select the type of certificate you need. The certificate will be generated and display in your browser. Login with your Credly's Acclaim platform credentials to view all your public badges. Learn more This will open in a new tab.

  • How did Myhub receive my data?

    The Clearinghouse received these education records as a trusted agent of your college or university. The education records we received are used to provide compliance, reporting, and administrative services to your institution and its students and alumni. Learn more This will open in a new tab.

  • Why can't I see data from all my colleges?

    Information is only displayed for colleges that participate in Myhub.
